The Process

How It Works

Simple, easy, and entirely online.

1

Fill Out the Intake Form

A short form collects your name, the service you want, and your date of birth. Takes two minutes. This helps Dalena prepare and tune in before your session.

2

Choose Your Appointment

After submitting the form, you're taken directly to Dalena's Google Calendar to pick a time that works for you. Available Monday–Friday 4pm–12am PT, Saturday 12pm–8pm PT.

3

Join via Zoom or Phone

At your session time, connect with Dalena over Zoom or by phone — whichever you prefer. No downloads required for phone sessions.

4

Get Clear on What's Next

Dalena opens the session by checking in on what you're carrying, then works through it with you — intuitively and practically. Sessions are conversational. You guide what matters most, she brings clarity.
