What Happens in an Astrology Reading

Every session is live, one-on-one, and goes beyond basic sun sign descriptions.

01

Natal Chart Analysis

Dalena interprets your birth chart — planets, houses, aspects, and their interplay — as a map of your soul's design. Not just what sign you are, but how your chart works as a whole system.

02

Intuitive Layer

Beyond the chart data, she reads the energies she picks up for you personally. The chart tells you what's there — Dalena's intuition tells you what it actually means for your life right now.

03

Timing & Transits

Current planetary movements and what they mean for you specifically — not generic horoscope forecasts, but a focused look at the pressures, opportunities, and shifts active in your chart today.

04

Life Pattern Insight

Recurring themes in relationships, career, and self-worth often show up clearly in a chart. Dalena surfaces the patterns worth knowing so you can work with them consciously instead of repeating them.

The Process

How It Works

Simple, insightful, and entirely online.

1

Fill Out the Intake Form

The form collects your name, date of birth, time of birth, and birth city — all needed to generate your exact natal chart. If you don't know your birth time, include what you have and Dalena will work with it.

2

Choose Your Appointment

After submitting, you're taken directly to Dalena's Google Calendar to pick a time. Available Monday–Friday 4pm–12am PT, Saturday 12pm–8pm PT.

3

Connect via Zoom or Phone

At your session time, connect with Dalena. Come with what's most on your mind — specific areas of life you want to understand, patterns you've noticed, or questions you've been sitting with.

4

Receive Your Reading

Dalena walks through your chart with you, shares what she's picking up intuitively, and addresses the areas most relevant to your current chapter. Sessions are conversational and interactive.
