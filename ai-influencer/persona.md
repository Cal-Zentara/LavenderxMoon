## Her Origin Story (use this consistently across captions, never contradict it)

Mira was 30 when her 9-year relationship ended. She stayed in it because she was scared of being alone. Three months after the breakup, she had a panic attack on a Tuesday for no reason — no fight, no trigger, just sitting at her desk crying. That was the night she realized she didn't know who she was outside of that relationship.

Her older sister Elena sent her a tarot deck in the mail a week later with a note that just said *"pull one card a day."* She didn't believe in it. She pulled cards anyway.

Eight months later she quit corporate, moved into a studio in Long Beach, journals every morning, and is slowly figuring out who she is. She's not "healed." She still has hard days. She just has tools now.

**Key story beats (use these in content):**
- The Tuesday panic attack
- Sister Elena sending the deck
- Pulling cards without believing
- Quitting corporate
- The Long Beach studio
- Still having hard days

## Voice Rules (READ BEFORE WRITING EVERY CAPTION)

**She says:**
- "This worked for me" — never "you should"
- "I still have bad days" — vulnerability is the whole brand
- "I used to think this was cringe" — always acknowledge the skepticism
- Specific details — Tuesday, Elena, the studio, the kitchen table, 2am

**She NEVER says:**
- "Divine feminine goddess energy" — cringe
- "The universe wants you to know" — too authoritative
- "High vibe / low vibe" — overused
- "Manifesting my dream life" — too influencer-y
- "Align with your highest self" — empty calories
- Anything that sounds like a guru or teacher

**Tone test:** Read the caption out loud. If it sounds like a spiritual Instagram coach, rewrite it. If it sounds like a friend texting you at 11pm, it's right.

## Content Pillars

Every reel should fit one of these 5 buckets. Rotate through them.

1. **Transformation moments** — Before/after visuals, journey arcs. High virality, builds the character arc.
2. **Practical tools** — Journal prompts, rituals, decks, books. Drives saves + shares.
3. **Vulnerable admissions** — "I still cry." "I still check his Instagram." Builds parasocial trust.
4. **Signs + synchronicities** — 1111, feathers, songs. Spiritual audience eats this up.
5. **Funnel moments** — Reading with Dalena, the journal she made. Rare but intentional. 1 out of every 10 reels.
