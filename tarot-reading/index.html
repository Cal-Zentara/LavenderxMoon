What Happens in a Tarot Reading

Every session is live, one-on-one, and completely personalized to you.

01

Intuitive Card Reading

Dalena reads the cards intuitively — not from a guidebook. What she sees and senses for you comes through the specific symbols, imagery, and energy present in your spread that day.

02

Direct Guidance

No vague generalizations. Dalena tells you what she's picking up clearly and directly — what's aligned, what's blocking you, and what your next move looks like energetically.

03

Open Q&A

Ask about love, career, life direction, relationships, or anything you're carrying. The session is yours — bring what's on your mind and leave with clarity on what matters most.

04

Energetic Awareness

Dalena blends tarot with her intuitive gifts — she may pick up on energy, emotions, or patterns she senses around your situation, adding depth beyond the cards alone.

The Process

How It Works

Simple, easy, and entirely online.

1

Fill Out the Intake Form

A short form collects your name, the service you want, and your date of birth. Takes two minutes. This helps Dalena prepare before your session.

2

Choose Your Appointment

After submitting the form, you're taken directly to Dalena's Google Calendar to pick a time that works for you. Available Monday–Friday 4pm–12am PT, Saturday 12pm–8pm PT.

3

Join via Zoom or Phone

At your session time, connect with Dalena over Zoom or by phone — whichever you prefer. No downloads required for phone sessions.

4

Receive Your Reading

Dalena opens intuitively and reads what's present for you. Sessions are conversational — you can ask questions throughout and guide the session toward what matters most.
