What the Reading Covers

This isn't a general reading. Each session is a structured, multi-dimensional look at who you are at soul level — and what's been shaping your experience across this lifetime and beyond.

01

Soul Pattern Mapping

Dalena reads the recurring themes and patterns your soul has carried — the tendencies, fears, gifts, and lessons that keep showing up across different areas of your life, often without a clear reason why.

02

Past-Life Influences

Some of what you're experiencing now has roots further back than this lifetime. Dalena identifies past-life energy that's still active and shaping your current relationships, blocks, or patterns.

03

Energetic Block Identification

Where are you stuck — and why? The reading surfaces specific energetic blocks that are creating resistance in your life, so you understand what's actually in the way, not just the surface-level symptoms.

04

Your Highest Path Forward

The session doesn't end at what's been. Dalena channels what's aligned for you going forward — the direction, decisions, and energy that will move you toward your highest potential in this chapter of life.

The Process

How It Works

A structured session — done entirely online, at a time that works for you.

1

Fill Out the Intake Form

A short form collects your name, date of birth, time of birth, and birth city. This information is essential — Dalena uses it to tune into your specific soul's energy before the session begins. Takes two minutes.

2

Choose Your Appointment

After submitting the form, you're taken directly to Dalena's Google Calendar to pick a time. Because Soul Blueprint sessions require significant preparation, book at least 24 hours in advance.

3

Dalena Prepares

Before your session, Dalena tunes into your energy using your birth details. She arrives already holding information — the session is focused and deep from the first minute, not general or exploratory.

4

Your Reading via Zoom or Phone

Dalena walks you through what she's received — the patterns, the past-life influences, the blocks, and what's aligned for you going forward. You can ask questions throughout and guide the session toward what resonates most.
