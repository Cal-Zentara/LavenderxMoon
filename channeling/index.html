What Happens in a Channeling Session

Every session is live, one-on-one, and completely unique to what comes through for you.

01

Spirit Guide Connection

Dalena opens a clear channel and receives direct communication from your spirit guides — the beings assigned to your soul's journey who hold a higher perspective on your path and purpose.

02

Higher Self Messages

Guidance from your own higher consciousness — the part of you that exists beyond the personality, holds your soul's full perspective, and knows what you most need to hear right now.

03

Loved Ones in Spirit

If you've lost someone and wish to receive a message, Dalena may be able to connect with those who have crossed over. This is not guaranteed but often comes through naturally during sessions.

04

Clear, Unfiltered Delivery

Messages are delivered exactly as received — Dalena doesn't reshape or soften what spirit communicates. You receive what's meant for you, even if it asks something of you.

The Process

How It Works

Simple, focused, and entirely online.

1

Fill Out the Intake Form

A short form collects your name, the service you want, and your date of birth. This helps Dalena tune in before the session and allows spirit to begin orienting to your energy.

2

Choose Your Appointment

After submitting, you're taken directly to Dalena's Google Calendar to pick a time. Available Monday–Friday 4pm–12am PT, Saturday 12pm–8pm PT.

3

Connect via Zoom or Phone

At your session time, connect with Dalena. Come with an open mind. If you have a specific person you hope to hear from, you can share that at the start — or simply let what wants to come through, come through.

4

Receive the Messages

Dalena opens the channel and delivers what comes through. Sessions are conversational — you can ask questions, request clarification, or follow a thread that resonates.
