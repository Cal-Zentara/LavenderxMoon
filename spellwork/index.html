What Happens in a Spellwork Session

Every session is personalized to your specific intention and situation.

01

Intention Clarity

Before any working begins, Dalena helps you identify exactly what you're calling in — and how to frame it for energetic alignment. A vague intention produces vague results. This step matters.

02

Ritual Design

She guides you through the elements, timing, and tools that will amplify your specific working — moon phases, herbs, crystals, candles, sigils, or any combination that fits your practice and what's available to you.

03

Ancestral & Elemental Wisdom

Dalena draws on both eastern and western lineages from her own ancestral background — grounding your work in real tradition rather than trend-based practice, and making it uniquely yours.

04

Follow-Through Guidance

Spellwork is a practice, not a one-time event. Dalena advises on how to tend your working after the session ends — what to watch for, how to stay in alignment, and when to refresh or close the working.

The Process

How It Works

Simple, grounded, and entirely online.

1

Fill Out the Intake Form

A short form collects your name, the service you want, and your date of birth. Takes two minutes. This helps Dalena begin sensing what approach will be most aligned for your specific situation.

2

Choose Your Appointment

After submitting, you're taken directly to Dalena's Google Calendar to pick a time. Available Monday–Friday 4pm–12am PT, Saturday 12pm–8pm PT.

3

Connect via Zoom or Phone

At your session time, come ready to talk openly about what you're working toward. The more honest you are about what you actually want, the more effective the guidance can be.

4

Leave With a Clear Working

By the end of the session, you'll have a clear, personalized ritual or working — exactly what to do, when to do it, and how to sustain it. No guesswork.
